EXCESS ENDORPHINS

(2016) (Direction) (Screenplay)

Excess Endorphins is a short film that depicts the feelings I experienced as a eenager in my first relationship. It represents loss and sadness, common emotions for someone with little emotional experience at that age.

This audiovisual project was presented at a high school student project festival, where it won awards for Best Screenplay and Best Production Design.

Thanks to this, I became interested in cinema and writting.
